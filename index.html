hello world, i'm

A Front-end Web Developer based out of Markham, Ontario, who has a diverse background as an Electrician, English teacher abroad in Japan, and Property Management. My love for tech and all things web-related has led me to finally pursue my passion for creating meaningful websites and web applications by tapping into my creativity.

During my time living in Japan, I grew a greater appreciation for the bridge between technology and its role in making meaningful impacts in people's lives. I knew that the future and my future lay in tech, and I had to be part of it.

As my first foray into the tech world; I write this bio as a Juno College Web Development Immersive Bootcamp grad!

I now strive to create and uphold accessible and responsive websites that can make a meaningful impact in your life... and your dog's life! Okay, maybe not so much the dog part.

When I'm not coding: you can find me on a frozen pond somewhere playing hockey, waiting patiently on G.R.R Martin and Patrick Rothfuss to release a book, practicing Japanese with my wife, and eating her delicious food. I am especially good at that last part.

Reach out to me if you think I would be a good fit into your team's world - let's make a difference!
